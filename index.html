We are Helion.

Helion is a FIRST Robotics team located at Dulwich College Beijing that was founded in 2018, with the purpose to successfully build and program a competition robot that will attend and compete in one of the FIRST Robotics regionals, located either in Shanghai or Shenzhen. Through growth and development, we hope to improve not only as individuals, but as a whole team. Our belief is that this experience will teach young students at Dulwich the perseverance and dedication needed to follow through on a path of their choice, carrying the spirit of the Helion team on into their future lives.

Our Mission Statement

We aim to remove the stigma around STEM whilst inspiring and developing young people, and engage them in worthwhile roles that will help develop engineering skills, improve entrepreneurial thinking, while inspiring a new level of innovation.

FAQ

What is the FRC?

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition that supplies students with a real world experience in engineering, programming and marketing. It has gained widespread recognition from international institutions in recent years, with 81 million USD in scholarships being offered by a wide selection of top universities and firms. Last year, the FIRST Robotics Club counted 3,647 competing teams with as many as 91,000 students and 25,000 mentors representing 27 different countries around the world. What is DCB?

Dulwich College Beijing (or DCB) is an international school located in Beijing, China. It is a very international school, with students from all around the world learning in its International English Curriculum. Find out more about this school here

What are community days?

Community days are events where we promote STEM Diversity to our community through robot showcases. We also plan to interact with junior school/primary school and early years/preschool to build strong STEM roots.
