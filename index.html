We are Helion.

We're a FIRST Robotics team that was founded in 2018 and is located at Dulwich College Beijing. We hope to successfully build and program a competition robot to attend and compete in one of the FIRST Robotics regionals, at either Shanghai or Shenzhen. Through growth and development, we hope to improve as not only individuals, but as a team. We believe that this will be an experience that will teach young people the perseverance needed to follow through on a path of their choosing, carrying this spirt onto the road to adulthood.

Our Mission Statement

We aim to remove the stigma around STEM whilst inspiring and developing young people, by engaging them in worthwhile roles that builds engineering skills, improves entrepreneurial thinking, and inspires innovation.

FAQ

What is the FRC?

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition that gives students real world engineering, programming and marketing experience. It has gained widespread recognition from international institutions in recent years (with 81 million USD in scholarships available from a wide selection of top universities and firms). Last year, FRC boasted 3,647 attending teams with more than 91,000 students and 25,000 mentors from 27 different countries.Find out more here

What is DCB?

DCB, or the Dulwich College Beijing, is an international school based in Beijing, China. This means that it has students from all around the world learning from an English curriculum in Beijing. Find out more here

What are community days?

Community days are events where we promote STEM Diversity to our community through robot showcases. We also plan to interact with junior school/primary school and early years/preschool to build strong STEM roots.
